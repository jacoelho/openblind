        I have been working at Form3 Financial Cloud full-time for less than a year

        Pros

        I joined Form3 around 3 months ago and have waited to write this review as I wanted it to be as realistic as possible. Some observations: - Overall à very positive place to work . - Everyone is friendly, supportive and collaborative. You’d never get turned down by a colleague if you need help. - You’d be valued and cherished. - Direct access to management where needed and they always have their doors opens - Flexible working with remote first work culture. The company culture is built to support everyone and within business work hours unless the job description suggests otherwise. - Transparency and candid approach to issues and matters in the business. - Opportunity to learn and work with the best in the industry - You get a MacBook Pro with an iPhone as work tools or a nice spec Dell Linux machine if you are a dev.

        Cons

        Not sure if I’d class this as a Con but like any growing business the pace is quite fast and that translates eventually into your individual thirst for growth. The other area is benefits, it’s not because the business isn’t working on it, but wants to have equal playing field for all colleagues in the business and same level of benefits. Still you get excellent holidays (30 days) and full access to Udemy. Also pension and share options, which is great!
